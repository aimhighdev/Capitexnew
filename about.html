Our Story

Built to Bridge the Wealth Gap

Our Mission

Structured Wealth-Building, Starting at $250

Traditional portfolio management firms set minimum investments that lock out most individual investors. We built Capitex to change that — giving anyone access to the same diversified, professionally managed strategies used by institutional investors.
