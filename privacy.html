Privacy Policy

We take your privacy seriously. This policy explains exactly what data we collect, why, and how we protect it.

Last updated: January 1, 2025 · Applies to all users of the Capitex platform and website.

1. Who We Are

Capitex Finance Ltd ("Capitex," "we," "us") operates the investment platform at capitex.finance. This Privacy Policy describes how we collect, use, store, and protect personal information gathered through our website, mobile interface, and investor dashboard.

2. Information We Collect

We collect the following categories of personal data when you interact with Capitex:

  • Identity data: First name, last name, date of birth, government-issued ID (collected during KYC verification)
  • Contact data: Email address, phone number, country of residence
  • Financial data: Deposit amounts, transaction history, withdrawal requests, selected investment plan
  • Technical data: IP address, browser type, device identifiers, login timestamps
  • Usage data: Pages visited, features used, session duration on the platform
  • Communications: Messages sent via contact forms, chat widget, or email to our team

3. How We Use Your Information

We use your personal data to: create and manage your investor account; process deposits, credits, and withdrawal requests; comply with KYC and AML regulatory obligations; send you account notifications, payout confirmations, and service updates; respond to your enquiries and support requests; improve and personalise the platform experience; and detect and prevent fraudulent activity.

4. Legal Basis for Processing

We process your data under the following legal bases: performance of a contract (account management and transactions); legal obligation (KYC/AML compliance); legitimate interests (fraud prevention, platform security, product improvement); and consent (marketing communications, where applicable). You may withdraw consent at any time.

5. KYC Data Retention

Identity documents collected for KYC verification are retained for a minimum of 5 years from account closure, as required by anti-money laundering regulations in our operating jurisdictions. This retention obligation cannot be waived at the user's request.

6. Data Sharing

We do not sell your personal data. We share your information only with: Firebase (Google) for database and authentication services; Formspree for contact form processing; payment processors as required to execute transactions; regulatory authorities where legally required; and our compliance and legal advisors under strict confidentiality agreements.

7. International Transfers

Your data may be stored or processed outside your country of residence, including in the United States (Firebase infrastructure). Where data is transferred outside the UK/EEA, we ensure appropriate safeguards are in place, including Standard Contractual Clauses approved by the relevant authority.

8. Cookies

Our website uses essential cookies required for the platform to function (session management, security). We do not currently use advertising or tracking cookies. You may disable cookies in your browser settings, though this may impair platform functionality.

9. Your Rights

Depending on your jurisdiction, you have the right to: access the personal data we hold about you; correct inaccurate data; request deletion of your data (subject to regulatory retention obligations); restrict or object to certain processing; data portability; and lodge a complaint with your local data protection authority. To exercise your rights, email privacy@capitex.finance.

10. Data Security

We implement technical and organisational measures to protect your data, including: end-to-end encryption for data in transit; Firebase security rules restricting data access by user UID; regular security reviews; and staff training on data handling. No system is 100% secure — please use a strong password and never share your login credentials.

11. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify registered users by email at least 14 days before material changes take effect. The "last updated" date at the top of this page reflects the most recent version.

12. Contact Us

For privacy-related questions or to exercise your data rights, contact our Data Protection lead at privacy@capitex.finance or via our contact page.
